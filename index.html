Koska ymmärrämme löydämme paremman

Starmap on 2016 perustettu teknologia-, fintech, ja cleantech-alan suorahakuihin erikoistunut yritys. Starmapissa työskentelee 4 headhunteria erilaisilla taustoilla.​

Haemme asiantuntijoita ml. myynti ja markkinointi, sekä keski-ja ylimmän johdon henkilöitä.​ Löydämme paremman tekijän asiakkaamme tiimiin oman kokemuksemme ja toimialaymmärryksen ansiosta. Meillä haun vetää headhunter, jolla on kokemusta alasta ja tehtävästä. ​

Meidän tapamme

Understanding

Our headhunters have work in the fields we focus on and quite often in the same position. So we understand what skillsets the job needs, what requirements field has for the employee and what attracts them to the job.

Consistency

We have one mediator for the whole recruitment process to scout and verify the candidates and especially to communicate with all parties involved with the process.

Quality

We stand as your representatives when hunting your next talents. We take great pride in being your brand ambassadors in headhunt. We understand the field, teamwork and recruiting. We are experts in the field we scout so the impression your recruitment gives is of quality; invested and professional. We leave even those not chosen smiling for the opportunity.
